Quicken Download Desktop for Windows & Mac

Get Quicken Desktop software to manage your finances easily. This guide helps you download, install, activate, and update Quicken on your computer.

Download Quicken

Download Quicken Desktop

Follow these simple steps to download Quicken Desktop safely:

  • Open your web browser.
  • Visit the official Quicken website.
  • Sign in to your Quicken account.
  • Select your Windows or Mac version.
  • Click on the Download button.
  • Save the installer file on your computer.

Install Quicken on Your Computer

  • Locate the downloaded installer file.
  • Double-click to start installation.
  • Follow on-screen setup instructions.
  • Accept license agreement.
  • Complete installation process.
  • Launch Quicken Desktop.

Activate Quicken Desktop

  • Open Quicken Desktop.
  • Sign in with your Quicken ID.
  • Enter license or activation code if asked.
  • Verify subscription.
  • Complete activation.

Update Quicken Software

  • Open Quicken Desktop.
  • Click Help in the top menu.
  • Select Check for Updates.
  • Install available updates.
  • Restart Quicken.

Quicken Support & Help

If you face any issues with Quicken download, installation, or activation, try:

  • Restart your computer.
  • Check internet connection.
  • Re-download installer.
  • Update your operating system.
  • Contact official Quicken support if needed.
